About OpenKAT

The OpenKatStory

OpenKAT was developed during the Covid-19 pandemic for the Ministry of Health. It was involved in discovering possible data breaches in the CoronaCheck-App. OpenKAT has since been made a free open-source tool, and is available for everyone to use and further develope.

WHATis OpenKAT?


OpenKAT is a monitoring tool and vulnerability scanner.

What does it do?

  • Combines information from different sources and tools
  • Scans your network automatically
  • Broad analysis and vulnerability detection

WHOis OpenKAT for?


Perfect for any Organisation regardless of its size:

  • Works with complex or large systems
  • Detects vulnerabilities and configuration mistakes hiding somewhere

OpenKAT finds them before they are found.



HOWdoes OpenKAT work?


  • BOEFJES scan and collect information
  • BYTES store raw data and metadata
  • WHISKERS filter for relevant objects
  • OCTOPOES stores the filtered data
  • BITS analyse objects in the Octopoes and create findings

WHYuse OpenKAT?

Most security incedents are caused by:

  • Known vulnerabilities
  • Human error
  • Constant updates

OpenKAT is:

  • Open source: openly accessible and collaboratively developed
  • Customisable and adpative through plug-ins
  • Creates practical reports
  • Easy adaptation for new vulnerabilities
