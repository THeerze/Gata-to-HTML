About OpenKAT

OURStory

OpenKAT was developed during the Covid-19 pandemic for the Ministry of Health and was involved in discovering possible data breaches in the CoronaCheck-App. OpenKAT has since been made open-source, and is available for everyone to use and develop.

WHATis OpenKAT?


OpenKAT is a monitoring tool and vulnerability scanner.

What does it do?

  • Combines information from different sources and tools
  • Scans your network automatically
  • Broad analysis and vulnerability detection

WHOis OpenKAT for?



Perfect for your Organisation regardless of its size: works with complex or large systems detects vulnerabilities and configuration mistakes OpenKAT finds them before they are found.



HOWdoes OpenKAT work?


BOEFJES scan and collect information BYTES stores raw data and metadata WHISKERS filters for relevant objects OCTOPOES stores the filtered data BITS Analyses objects in the Octopoes and creates findings

WHYuse OpenKAT?

Most security incedents are caused by:

  • Known vulnerabilities
  • Human error
  • Constant updates

OpenKAT is:

  • Open source: openly accessible and collaborative development
  • Customisable and adpative through plug-ins
  • Creates practical reports
  • Easy adaptation for new vulnerabilities
